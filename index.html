PART II — Why We Disconnect From Ourselves

Self-Abandonment

We disconnect from ourselves when we leave our own feelings, needs, and inner child behind.

Self-Abandonment Patterns
  • ✓Seeking love, acceptance, admiration and approval from others.
  • ✓Taking care of others' needs and wants more than our own.
  • ✓Putting others in charge of meeting our needs.
  • ✓Feeling worthy and good enough ONLY when we achieve something.
  • ✓Distracting ourselves to avoid uncomfortable feelings and the pain of loneliness.
  • ✓Using the same mechanisms from childhood to gain love, acceptance and feel secure.
